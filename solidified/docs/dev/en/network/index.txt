# The Network Module

This is where you get an overview of what social activities are going on.

## Activity-Filters

You can filter, sort and filter posts from certain contacts, by duration, tags or text.

There are three sort options: 
- **Latest**: This sorts posts by the date they were created, i.e. latest posts first.
- **Active**: This puts the most recently commented/liked/disliked post first. 
- **All**: Default sort. 

Star Filter: This filters the stared posts. Can be used in conjunction with other filters. For example, starred posts of a particular connection, or starred DMs, starred posts within a certain date range, etc.

Personal Filter: This filters all posts wwhere you have commented on, liked, disliked, repeated. Can be used with other filters.

DM Filter: Shows the DMs. Can be used along other filters.

Filter by Connection: Filters the posts of a particular connection. 

Search: Use this to search posts with a certain text in it. 

More filters: Clickingon the filter button opens more filter options:
- Tag: Filters posts with a certain tag.
- From date: Filters posts made after a given date.
- To date: Filters posts made upto a given date. 
- Min. Affinity: lower cutoff for the affinity filter.
- Max. Affinity: upper limit for the affinity filter.
