# Accounts

The Accounts section lists every registered account on your hub. Use it to review, block, or remove accounts.

[IMAGE: Accounts table with email, channels, created date, status, and action columns]

## The Accounts Table

Each row shows:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Email** | The account's registration email address |
| **Channels** | How many channels this account owns |
| **Created** | When the account was registered |
| **Status** | Blank = active; **Blocked** badge = account is blocked |
| **Actions** | Block / Unblock / Delete buttons |

## Blocking an Account

Click **Block** on an account row. The account is immediately suspended — the user cannot log in and their content is hidden from public pages.

A blocked account shows a red **Blocked** badge in the Status column. Click **Unblock** to restore access.

**Use blocking when:** a user violates your terms but you want to preserve the ability to unblock them or review their content later.

[IMAGE: Blocked account row with Blocked badge and Unblock button]

## Deleting an Account

Click **Delete** on an account row. A confirmation dialog appears:

> **Delete this account? This cannot be undone.**

Confirm to permanently remove the account, all its channels, all their posts, and all associated data from your hub. Federation deletion notices are sent to remote servers.

**Use deletion for:** spam accounts, accounts that are clearly not legitimate, or at a user's own request.

> ⚠ Deletion is irreversible. Block first if you are unsure.

## Pagination

The table shows accounts in pages. Use the **Previous** and **Next** buttons below the table to navigate. The header line shows your current position (e.g. "Showing 1–20 of 347").

[IMAGE: Pagination controls below the accounts table]

## Pending Registrations

If your registration policy is set to "with approval", new account applications appear in the **Summary** section under "Pending registrations". Approve or deny them there (link to the relevant Hubzilla admin page or workflow).
