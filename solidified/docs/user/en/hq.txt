# HQ — Your Dashboard

HQ is your personal home base. It is the first page you see after logging in. It gives you a quick overview of what's happening and lets you post without navigating elsewhere.

![IMAGE: Full HQ page with all widgets visible](hq/hq.png)

## Post Composer

The composer at the top of HQ lets you write and publish a new post directly.

![IMAGE: HQ composer in its collapsed state](hq/composer-collapsed.png)

- Click the text area to expand the full composer.
- Write your post using plain text or BBCode formatting.
- Use the **toolbar** to quickly insert bold, italic, links, images, and other BBCode tags.
- Choose who can see your post with the **privacy selector** (see [composing-posts.txt](composing-posts.txt) for details).
- Your draft is saved automatically as you type — if you close the tab by accident it will still be there.

![IMAGE: Composer expanded with toolbar and privacy picker visible](hq/composer-full.png)

## Drafts Widget

Unfinished posts are saved as drafts. The **Drafts** widget (right column on HQ) shows your saved drafts. Click a draft to continue editing it.

![IMAGE: Drafts widget showing a list of draft titles](hq/drafts.png)

## Upcoming Events Widget

Shows your next events from the Calendar app — anything in the next 30 days. Each event shows the date, month, time, and title.

- Click the **+** button to create a new event directly from HQ.
- Click an event title to open the full calendar view.

![IMAGE: Upcoming events widget with event list and create button](hq/upcoming_events.png)

## Messages Widget

Shows recent private direct messages. Click a message to open the full conversation in the Network view (DM filter).

[IMAGE: Messages widget showing message previews]

## Performance Widget

Admin-only. Shows live server performance: load average (1/5/15 min), database query rate, and outbound/work queue sizes, each with a short rolling sparkline. Polls every few seconds; not shown to non-admin users.

![Performance widget with sparkline cards](hq/perfcard.png)

## Keyboard Tip

Press **Enter** (or **Ctrl+Enter** depending on settings) to submit a post from the composer. The exact behaviour is set in Display Settings.
