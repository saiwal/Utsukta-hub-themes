# Cart

The Cart app lets a channel sell items and lets visitors buy them. It adds a shopping cart to your sidebar and, for the channel owner, a lightweight view of incoming orders.

## Shopping Cart Widget

Shown in the sidebar wherever the Cart app is relevant.

- **As a buyer**, it shows a running summary of items you've added, with a link to check out.
- **As the channel owner**, it also shows counts of unpaid and unfulfilled orders, so you can keep track of sales that need attention.

## Item Card Widget

An opt-in showcase card that highlights one specific item from the catalog you choose, with a quick **add to cart** action. Add it from the widget picker, then use its gear icon to pick the item (by SKU); add several copies to feature more than one product.
