# Articles

The Articles app lets you write, publish, and read long-form content — similar to a blog. Articles are separate from regular stream posts and have their own listing page.

Navigate to `/articles/<nick>` to see a channel's articles.

[IMAGE: Articles listing page with article cards]

## Reading Articles

The articles listing shows cards for each published article:
- **Title**
- **Summary / excerpt**
- **Author** and **publish date**
- **Category** and **tags** (if set)

Click an article card to read the full article.

[IMAGE: Full article view with title, body, author, and date]

## Right Sidebar Widgets

The Articles sidebar shows three widgets:

### Popular Articles
A list of the most-read or most-liked articles on this channel.

[IMAGE: Popular articles widget]

### Categories
A list of article categories. Click a category to filter the listing.

[IMAGE: Categories widget]

### Tags
A tag cloud showing all tags used across articles. Click a tag to filter.

[IMAGE: Tags widget]

## Writing an Article

If you are the channel owner, look for a **New article** or **Write** button. Articles support full BBCode formatting plus titles, summaries, and categories.

[IMAGE: Article editor with title, summary, and body fields]

## Visiting Someone Else's Articles

Navigate to `/articles/<their-nick>` to see their published articles. Only public articles are visible to visitors.

> **Note:** The Articles app must be installed on the channel for the tab and `/articles/<nick>` route to be active.

## Drafts Widget

Shown only to you, on your own Articles page. Lists articles you've started but not published, so you can pick one up where you left off or discard it. Never shown to visitors.

## Popular Articles Widget

A list of the channel's most-read or most-liked articles, letting visitors jump straight to the highlights.

## Categories Widget

Lists the categories used across this channel's articles. Click one to filter the listing.

## Tags Widget

A tag cloud of hashtags used across this channel's articles, sized by frequency. Click a tag to filter the listing.

## Teaser Widget

An opt-in showcase card that highlights one specific article you choose — title and excerpt, with a link to read it in full. Add it from the widget picker, then use its gear icon to pick the article; add several copies to feature more than one.
