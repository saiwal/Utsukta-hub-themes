# Wiki

The Wiki app provides collaborative, editable pages attached to a channel. It is useful for documentation, project notes, FAQs, or any content that needs to grow over time.

Navigate to `/wiki/<nick>` to see a channel's wikis.

[IMAGE: Wiki list page showing available wiki names]

## Wiki Structure

A channel can have **multiple wikis** — each wiki is like a mini-site with its own set of pages.

- The **Wiki list** (`/wiki/<nick>`) shows all wikis for that channel.
- Click a wiki name to open it and see its pages.
- The **Pages view** lists all pages in the wiki.
- Click a page title to read it.

[IMAGE: Wiki pages listing within a single wiki]

## Reading a Wiki Page

Wiki pages are written in BBCode or Markdown and rendered as formatted HTML. A breadcrumb trail at the top shows where you are.

[IMAGE: Single wiki page with rendered content and breadcrumb]

## Editing Pages

If you have permission (channel owner or granted editor), click **Edit** on a page to modify its content. Changes are saved immediately on submit.

[IMAGE: Wiki page edit view with text editor]

## Creating New Pages

Use the **New page** button (if you have permission) to add a page to the current wiki. Give it a title and write the content.

> **Note:** The Wiki app must be installed on the channel. If you don't see the Wiki tab on a channel, the app is not active there.
