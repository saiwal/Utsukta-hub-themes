# Navigation

Hubzilla's navigation lives in three places: the primary nav list (the left sidebar on desktop, or the bottom tab bar and "More" drawer on mobile), the account/utility row beneath it, and a couple of dedicated buttons on mobile. This page explains what each link or button does — for how to actually use what's behind it, see that app's own help page.

## Hq

Your personal home base after logging in — a dashboard combining your composer, upcoming events, recent messages, and drafts in one place.

## Network

Your stream: posts from everyone you're connected to, with filters for stars, mentions, direct messages, saved searches, and more.

## Channel

Your own channel wall and profile — what visitors see when they view your page.

## Photos

Your photo albums and the image viewer.

## Files

Cloud file storage for uploading, organizing, and sharing files.

## Articles

The Articles app, for writing and reading long-form content.

## Wiki

Collaborative wiki pages you and your connections can edit.

## Calendar

Your calendar and events, including RSVPs.

## Chat

Chatrooms you've joined or created.

## Webpages

Static pages you build and attach to your channel.

## Notepad

A private, personal scratchpad only you can see.

## Bookmarks

Posts and pages you've bookmarked for later.

## Directory

Search and browse other channels on this site and across the network.

## Profile

Your profile details — the "about" information visitors see on your channel.

## Cart

Your shopping cart, if this site has the Cart app enabled.

## Games

Puzzle games built into Hubzilla.

## Tools

Miscellaneous utilities bundled with your account.

## Pubstream

The public stream — public posts from across the site, visible without logging in.

## Siteinfo

Information about this Hubzilla site: version, admin contact, and terms.

## Help

Opens the help guide's table of contents.

## Admin

Site administration. Only visible to site admins.

## Settings

All of your account settings: display, profile, privacy, notifications, and more.

## Manage

Switch between the channels you own, if you manage more than one.

## Login

Sign in to your account.

## Logout

Sign out of your account.

## Register

Create a new account on this site.

## Remote Login

Log in to your home site from this one, so you can interact here using your own identity.

## Account Menu

Click your avatar and name to reveal account actions: settings, channel management, and logout.

## Language

Switch the interface language.

## Theme

Switch between light, dark, and other color themes.

## Help Mode

Toggles help mode. While it's on, click anything on the page — a nav link, a widget, a button — to see what it does.

## More Drawer

Mobile only. Opens a drawer with any nav items and account actions that don't fit in the bottom tab bar.

## Panel

Opens the right-hand sidebar panel — the widgets you'd otherwise see beside the page on desktop.
