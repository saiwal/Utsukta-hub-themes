# Notifications

The notifications widget lives in the **right sidebar** on most pages. It keeps you informed about activity on your posts, new connections, messages, and upcoming events.

[IMAGE: Notifications widget in the right sidebar with a list of items]

## Notification Types

| Icon | Event |
|------|-------|
| 💬 | Someone commented on your post |
| 👍 | Someone liked your post |
| 🔁 | Someone repeated your post |
| 👤 | New connection request received |
| ✅ | Connection request accepted |
| ✉ | New private message |
| 📅 | Upcoming event / event today |
| 🎂 | Upcoming birthday |
| ⚙ | System notification |

## Reading Notifications

- **Unread** notifications are highlighted.
- Click a notification to go directly to the relevant post, message, or event.
- Click **Mark all read** (or the tick icon at the top of the widget) to clear all unread highlights at once.

[IMAGE: Notifications widget with unread items highlighted and "Mark all read" button]

## Notification Badge

When you have unread notifications, a badge count appears on the notification bell icon in the top bar or bottom tab bar.

[IMAGE: Nav bar with unread notification badge]

## Configuring Notifications

Go to **Settings → Notifications** to choose which events trigger:
- **Email notifications** (sent to your account email)
- **Visual notifications** (shown in the widget and badge)

See [settings.txt](settings.txt) for the full list of notification options.
